About

This application utilizes the YOLOv8 model to predict and classify objects in images uploaded by users. Here's how it works:

  1. Users can upload images containing various objects.
  2. The YOLOv8 model processes the uploaded image and detects objects in it.

It then classifies the objects into one of the following categories:

  • Coin
  • Bar
  • Mangalsutra
  • Thali
  • Others

Once the processing is complete, the application displays the results to the user.

Experience the power of object detection with our easy-to-use application!
